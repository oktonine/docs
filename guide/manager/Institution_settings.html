# Institute profile

Ta access your institute's settings, click on the name of your institute, then on settings.

Within the different tabs, you will find all your institute's data:

  • A: General information
  • B: Projects
  • C: Methodologies
  • D: Competency Frameworks
  • E: Users
  • F: Departments
  • G: Partners
  • H: Resources
  • I: Showrooms
